⚖️ Constraint System
🔴 Hard Constraints
⛔
No Overlapping Shifts
An employee cannot be assigned two shifts on the same day and time period.
⛔
Skill Requirements
Each shift has a minimum required skill level. Only qualified employees can be assigned.
⛔
Availability Windows
Employees can only be assigned on days they are marked available.
⛔
Maximum Weekly Hours
Assignments cannot cause an employee to exceed their weekly hour cap.
🔵 Soft Constraints
💡
Fair Hour Distribution
Maximize equity across the team — minimizes max-min hour delta.
💡
Shift Preferences
Honor employee morning/afternoon/night preferences where possible.
💡
Balanced Night Rotation
Avoid assigning repeated consecutive night shifts to the same employee.
💡
Minimum Rest Gap
Enforce at least 8 hours of rest between consecutive shift ends.
